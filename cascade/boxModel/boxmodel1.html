THE TITLE

The Apple Tree and the Little Boy

Once upon a time, there was a small apple tree in a garden. A little boy visited the tree every day. He played around it, climbed its branches, and ate its sweet apples. The apple tree was very happy. As the boy grew older, he visited less often. One day, he came back and said, "I need money." The tree replied, "Take my apples and sell them." The boy picked the apples and left. The tree was happy to help. Many years later, the boy returned as an old man. He said, "I am tired and need a place to rest." The tree said, "Sit under my shade and rest." The old man sat beside the tree peacefully, and the tree was happy once again. Moral: Kindness and giving make both the giver and receiver happy.
